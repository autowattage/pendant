your pendant should...

  • be easy to carry around!

    the maximum size for your pendant is 4". aside from saving hardware costs, keychains are best left small and portable.

  • look pretty!

    a 3d printed case is optional, but your pendant should be polished so that you'll want to wear it around and show everyone! silkscreen art is highly reccomended.

  • have a keychain hole!

    so you can attach the lanyard to it :p

ready to start?

  1. make a pcb

    new to pcb making? check out hackpad and blueprint's starter projects/resources.

  2. design the case

    optional step, but you'll earn an additional keychain for making a case!

  3. document your work!

    to 'track' your hours, make a journal.md in your code repository going over your design process! make sure to update it regularly and take photos!

    you'll also want to add a BOM (bill of materials) to show what you'll be using your hardware grant on.

  4. ship your project

  5. get your prizes!

    every project earns a lanyard and $5usd/hour towards a hardware grant.
    projects with custom 3d printed cases can earn an additional keychain!
